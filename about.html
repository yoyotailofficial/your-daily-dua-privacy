About the App – Your Daily Dua

Last updated: 2026-01-15

Your Daily Dua is a simple, offline-first Islamic supplication app designed to help Muslims remember and read authentic duas in daily life. The content is based on Islamic teachings and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and references are included where applicable.

The app does not require accounts or login. It focuses on clarity, authenticity, and ease of use.

What You Will Find in the App

  • Authentic duas presented with Arabic text, Latin transliteration, and meaning/translation.
  • References shown within the app where applicable (e.g., hadith sources).
  • Categories and tags to make it easier to find relevant duas.
  • Offline access so you can read duas anytime without internet.

Content & Authenticity

The application is built around a commitment to authenticity:

  • Duas are sourced from recognized Islamic references.
  • The app does not intentionally add, modify, or fabricate religious content.
  • Where available, references are displayed to support transparency.

If you notice a possible typo or believe a reference needs correction, please contact the developer.

How the App Is Funded

Your Daily Dua is supported by banner ads provided through Google AdMob. This helps keep the app free for everyone.

No login is required, and the developer does not collect or sell personal data. Limited technical/advertising data may be processed by AdMob according to Google’s policies.
